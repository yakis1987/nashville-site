Why We Listed Her

"Darlin' Cait has a way with traditional tattooing that is immediately recognizable. Her color work is vibrant without being garish, and her black and grey reads with the same confident line quality. She trained under Kustom Thrills veterans and it shows in every piece she puts out."

Cait's American Traditional work carries the graphic authority the style demands, with a color sensibility that feels personal rather than by-the-book. She has internalized the tradition and makes it her own.
Her black and grey is not an afterthought. The same strong linework that defines her color pieces carries over, producing tonal work with real visual weight and longevity.
As a mentor to apprentices at Kustom Thrills, Cait brings genuine teaching discipline to her craft. Artists who teach tend to be more precise, more consistent, and more thoughtful about the fundamentals.

The Artist

MEET Darlin' Cait

DARLIN' CAIT
American Traditional · Bold Color · Black and Grey · Custom

Darlin' Cait is a Nashville tattoo artist based at Kustom Thrills Riverside in East Nashville. She has pursued tattooing with singular focus from the start of her career, building a reputation for American Traditional work that is bold, clean, and built to age. Her color palette is confident and distinctive. Her black and grey carries the same strong lines. Cait also mentors apprentices at Kustom Thrills, which reflects the seriousness she brings to her craft and the depth of her technical foundation. If Dolly Parton got into tattooing, she would be Darlin' Cait.

The Shop

KUSTOM Thrills

Kustom Thrills is one of East Nashville's defining tattoo shops, with two locations serving the neighborhood from 5 Points and Riverside Village. The Riverside location is home to Darlin' Cait and a team of artists who take traditional tattooing seriously.

The shop has been a fixture of the East Nashville creative community for years. It attracts clients who know what they want and want it done right. Open daily from noon to 7pm, no appointment required for walk-in flash.
