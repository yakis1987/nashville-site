The Artist

MEET Natasha

NATASHA RACHEL
Color Fine Line · Botanical · Mandalas · Whimsical · Freehand

Natasha Rachel is a female fine line tattoo artist based in Nashville, originally from England. Working full time in the industry since 2017, her path into tattooing was shaped by a background in photography and years managing tattoo shops in London. She draws inspiration from architecture, patterns, and the people who sit in her chair — every consultation becomes the starting point for something new. She works exclusively on custom pieces, designed from scratch for every client, priced by piece so there are never surprises.
