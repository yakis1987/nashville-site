Why We Listed Jake

"Jake Ingersoll is one of those artists who makes realism look effortless. His command of both black and grey and full color sets him apart — whether it's a cinematic sleeve or an intimate portrait, the level of craft is immediately apparent."

Jake specializes in hyper-realistic black and grey and color realism — the kind of work that stops people mid-scroll and holds up for decades on skin.
His ability to render texture, light, and depth — whether on a film character, a portrait, or an action scene — reflects years of serious technical development.
Every client goes through a dedicated consultation process: design, placement, and pricing are handled with professionalism before a single needle touches skin.
Jake's track record speaks for itself — complex, large-scale pieces executed with precision and care, from first appointment through to aftercare.
