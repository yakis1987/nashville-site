The Shop

CUSTOM Tattooing

Sunrise Tattoo is a custom tattoo shop located inside the Taylor Arts Collective in Nashville, Tennessee. The shop specializes in designs made to fit your body, drawn with care and tattooed to last a lifetime.

With four artists covering traditional, fine line, floral, Japanese, folk, European, and realism styles, Sunrise offers a breadth of craft that accommodates virtually any vision. Appointments are preferred, but walk-ins are welcome when availability allows.
