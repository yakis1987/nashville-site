Visitor Guide

GETTING TATTOOED in Nashville

🎸

NASHVILLE'S SCENE

Nashville's tattoo talent has grown alongside the city itself. From the honky-tonk strip to East Nashville's art communities, you'll find artists with formal fine arts backgrounds and deep craft knowledge working across every style.

📅

BOOK AHEAD

The best artists in Nashville book out weeks in advance. If you're planning a trip, reach out at least 2-4 weeks before arrival. Most artists offer free consultations to discuss your idea before committing to a deposit.

💡

WHAT TO EXPECT

Most custom work requires a deposit of $50-$100. Simple flash can often be done same-day at shops that accept walk-ins. Prices are generally quoted in person after discussing the design, placement, and complexity.

🌿

AFTERCARE

Nashville summers are hot and humid — both can affect healing. Keep fresh tattoos out of direct sun, avoid pools and natural water for 2-3 weeks, and follow your artist's specific aftercare instructions closely.

✦

CHOOSING AN ARTIST

Match the artist to the style you want. A fine line specialist and a traditional artist require completely different skills. Review portfolios carefully and look for healed work — not just fresh tattoos under studio lighting.

💳

FINANCING

Several Nashville artists now offer financing through Affirm or Klarna — meaning you can get world-class work without paying everything upfront. Ask when booking if financing is important to you.
