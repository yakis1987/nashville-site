The Artist

MEET Sophie

SOPHIE
Kawaii · Neo-Traditional · Illustrative · Color · UV Reactive · Pixel

Sophie is a tattoo artist and co-owner of Why Not? Tattoo Club, a woman-owned private tattoo shop in East Nashville. Known online as Someone's Weird Sister, her work spans an extraordinary range of styles, from cute kawaii characters and anime to bold neo-traditional, color micro-realism, UV reactive ink, and pixel art. Every piece reflects a deep love of pop culture, cartoons, and nostalgia, translated into tattoos that are joyful, precise, and built to last.

The Shop

PRIVATE Tattoo Shop

Why Not? Tattoo Club is a woman-owned, private tattoo shop in East Nashville. Co-owned and operated by Sophie, the shop offers an intimate, appointment-based experience designed to make every client feel at ease from the first conversation to the finished piece.

With a focus on custom work and a deep respect for each client's vision, the shop has built a loyal following for its creativity, consistency, and the sheer breadth of styles Sophie brings to the needle.
