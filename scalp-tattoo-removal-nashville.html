What Scalp Removal Actually Costs

Most scalp pieces are small, keeping total cost lower than larger placements.

Most scalp tattoos, script, small symbols, or thin small symbols or words, fall into the small pricing tier most Nashville clinics use. That generally means $100 to $160 per session, with 4 to 7 sessions needed for full clearance, putting a realistic total between $400 and $1,100 for a typical scalp piece.

A larger design or a scalp piece with denser shading moves into the medium tier, often $150 to $220 per session across 7 to 9 sessions, since there is simply more ink for the laser to break down. Very few scalp tattoos are large enough to reach the highest pricing tier, since the scalp itself is a naturally small treatment area.

How Much Does It Hurt on the Scalp?

The scalp sits in the moderate range of our pain by body area breakdown, since skin here sits close to the skull with limited padding, similar to other bonier spots on the body.

Because of that, numbing matters more on the scalp than on many other spots. Topical numbing cream applied 30 to 60 minutes before your session helps, and some clients ask about a cooling device or injectable numbing for added comfort. Full Removal vs Fading for a Cover-Up

Full clearance is realistic on the scalp with a consistent session schedule.

The scalp is one of the most common spots people plan a cover-up for, since it is visible and frequently gets a second piece of work. If your goal is new art rather than bare skin, our partial removal guide covers how targeted fading, usually 2 to 5 sessions instead of a full course, can prepare the area for a cover-up artist to work with.

If you are set on completely clear skin with no new tattoo planned, budget for the full session count above and see our consultation guide for what to expect at your first appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to remove a scalp tattoo in Nashville?
Most scalp tattoos are small designs, running roughly $100 to $160 per session across 4 to 7 sessions, for a realistic total between $400 and $1,100. A larger design costs more.
Does scalp tattoo removal hurt more than other spots?
The scalp is moderately sensitive, since skin here sits close to the skull with limited padding, similar to other bonier spots on the body. Most clients find a standard numbing routine sufficient.
How many sessions does a scalp tattoo typically need?
Most small scalp tattoos need 4 to 7 sessions. A larger design or a piece with denser shading can take 7 to 9 sessions.
Can a scalp tattoo be partially removed for a cover-up instead?
Yes. Small symbols or words and small scalp designs are common candidates for partial fading, since 2 to 5 sessions of targeted fading is often enough to prepare the area for a new design.
Is numbing more important for scalp removal than other spots?
Yes, given the sensitivity here. Topical numbing cream helps most clients, and some ask about a cooling device or injectable numbing for extra comfort on this specific area.
Can I easily cover a scalp tattoo during healing?
It depends on your hair coverage. With enough hair, the treated area is easy to cover, but shorter styles or thinning hair leave it more exposed, which is worth planning around during a course of treatment.
Can laser removal affect hair growth on the scalp?
It is a reasonable question to ask your technician. Laser treatment targets ink pigment specifically, but discussing hair growth expectations for your exact situation during a consultation is worth doing before starting.
