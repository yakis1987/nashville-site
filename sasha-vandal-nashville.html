MEET Sasha

SASHA VANDAL
American Traditional · Bold Color · Black & Grey · Custom

Sasha Vandal is a Nashville tattoo artist with a style that is immediately his own. His work lives at the intersection of American Traditional and something harder to define — bold outlines, unexpected color palettes, and a compositional instinct shaped by years spent in Nashville's art and music scenes. Every tattoo he makes carries that influence: graphic, confident, and built to age well. Whether you come in with a clear vision or just a rough direction, Sasha brings genuine creative investment to the process and makes sure you leave with something you're proud to wear for life.
